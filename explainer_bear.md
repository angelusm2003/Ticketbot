## Problem Statement

IT service desks are overwhelmed by poorly structured, incomplete support tickets that slow down resolution times and frustrate both users and engineers. TicketBot solves this by letting users describe their issue in plain English through an AI chat interface, which automatically extracts key details — summary, priority, issue type, and description — and generates a structured Jira ticket in seconds. This eliminates manual triage, reduces back-and-forth, and ensures every ticket is complete and actionable from the start.
